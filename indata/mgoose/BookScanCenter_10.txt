PAT-A-CAKE

Pat-a-cake, pat-a-cake,
Baker's man!

So I do, master,
As fast as I can.

Pat it, and prick it,
And mark it with T,
Put it in the oven
For Tommy and me.

MONEY AND THE MARE

“Lend me thy mare to ride a mile.”
“She is lamed, leaping over a stile."

“Alack! and I must keep the fair!
I’ll give thee money for thy mare.”

“Oh, oh.’ say you so?
Money will make the mare to go!"
